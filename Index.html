Student Led Initiative

EBM Daily is a student led initiative formed by MBBS BDS nursing and veterinary science students. It is built on the principles of evidence based medicine rational thinking and scientific integrity.

Anonymous Collaboration

All contributions are anonymous. Students contribute articles videos research explanations and educational content without fear hierarchy or institutional pressure. Only evidence matters.

Our Purpose

We aim to counter medical misinformation simplify complex science and promote evidence based medical awareness for the general public using clear and accessible language.
